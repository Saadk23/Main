## 🧠 Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?

### Simple Explanation

Think of it like this:

1. **You ask a question** (Tum sawal poochte ho)
2. **Robot searches in documents** (Robot documents mein dhoondta hai)
3. **AI creates answer** (AI jawab banata hai)
4. **You get the answer** (Tumhe jawab milta hai)

### Example

**Aap:** "Pool ki timings kya hain?"

**Behind the scenes:**
1. Robot tumhara sawal samajhta hai
2. Society ke documents mein "pool" aur "timings" dhoondta hai
3. Sahi information nikalti hai
4. Achi tarah se jawab bana kar deti hai

**Tumko:** "Swimming pool 7 AM se 9 PM tak khula rehta hai..."
