# 🔑 Visitor QR Code & Entry System

The society uses a secure digital entry system to manage guests and service providers efficiently.

## 1. Generating a Guest Pass
1. Open the **Society Smart App**.
2. Select **'Invite Guest'**.
3. Enter Guest Name and Date of Visit.
4. Share the generated **QR Code** via WhatsApp with your guest.

## 2. Entry Protocol
* Guests must show the QR code to the scanner at the main gate.
* For guests without a smartphone, residents must call the **Security Gate (Ext: 555)** for manual verification.
