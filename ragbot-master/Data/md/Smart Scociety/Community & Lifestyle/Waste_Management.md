# ♻️ Solid Waste & Sanitation Rules

Framework for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene within the society.

## 1. Garbage Collection Schedule
* **Daily Collection:** Between 08:00 AM and 11:00 AM.
* **Segregation:** Residents must separate dry waste from wet waste in designated bags.

## 2. Prohibited Acts
* A fine of **Rs. 500** will be imposed for littering on the streets.
* Dumping debris or garbage in vacant plots carries a **Rs. 5,000** fine plus the cost of cleanup.
