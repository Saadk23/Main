# 🏠 Society Guest House (Executive Suites)

For residents who have guests but limited space at home, the society offers premium lodging facilities.

## 1. Room Categories & Rates
| Room Type | Amenities | Daily Rate (Residents) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Suite** | Queen Bed, Wi-Fi, Breakfast | Rs. 5,000 |
| **Executive Suite** | King Bed, Mini-bar, Lounge Access | Rs. 8,000 |
| **Family Suite** | 2 Bedrooms + Kitchenette | Rs. 12,000 |

## 2. Booking Policy
* **Verification:** Guests must be accompanied by the host resident at the time of check-in.
* **Advance Notice:** Minimum 48 hours notice is required for guaranteed availability.
* **Duration:** Maximum stay allowed is 7 consecutive days.
