# ♻️ Community Recycling & Eco-Hub

The society is committed to a "Zero Waste" goal through our advanced on-site recycling facility.

## 1. Waste Segregation Guidelines
* **Green Bins:** For organic and kitchen waste (converted into compost for society parks).
* **Blue Bins:** For recyclable dry waste like plastic, paper, and metal.
* **Red Bins:** For hazardous waste like batteries, chemicals, and electronic scrap.

## 2. Resident Incentives
| Waste Type | Quantity | Reward (Maintenance Credit) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Plastic Bottles | 10 Units | Rs. 50 |
| Old Newspapers | 5 kg | Rs. 100 |
| Used Cooking Oil | 1 Litre | Rs. 30 |

* **Note:** Residents who consistently segregate waste correctly are eligible for the "Green Citizen" monthly award.
