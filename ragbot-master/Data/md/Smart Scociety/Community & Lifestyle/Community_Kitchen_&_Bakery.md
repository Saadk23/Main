# 🍳 Community Kitchen & Bakery

A centralized hygienic kitchen facility that provides fresh meals and bakery items exclusively for residents.

## 1. Services Provided
* **Live Bakery:** Fresh bread, cakes, and pastries available every morning at 07:00 AM.
* **Lunch/Dinner Tiffin:** Residents can subscribe to weekly or monthly meal plans delivered to their doorstep.
* **Cooking Classes:** Weekend workshops for culinary enthusiasts (Schedule updated on the App).

## 2. Hygiene & Quality Standards
* **Certifications:** All staff undergo monthly health checkups and possess food safety certifications.
* **Feedback:** Residents can rate the meal quality via the Society App to ensure continuous improvement.
