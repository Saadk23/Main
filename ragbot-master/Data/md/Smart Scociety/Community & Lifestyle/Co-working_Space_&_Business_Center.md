# 💻 Co-working Space & Business Center

To support freelancers and remote workers, the society offers a professional workspace with high-speed internet.

## 1. Facilities & Equipment
* **Dedicated Desks:** Quiet workstations with ergonomic seating.
* **Meeting Rooms:** Soundproof rooms for video conferences and team meetings (Advance booking required).
* **Office Services:** High-speed Wi-Fi, printing, scanning, and complimentary tea/coffee.
