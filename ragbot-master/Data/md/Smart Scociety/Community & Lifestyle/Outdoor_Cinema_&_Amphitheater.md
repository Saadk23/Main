# 🎬 Outdoor Cinema & Amphitheater

A premium open-air venue for movie screenings, cultural performances, and community theater.

## 1. Weekly Schedule
* **Family Movie Night:** Every Friday at 08:00 PM (Complimentary for residents).
* **Cultural Shows:** Monthly live music or theater performances (Tickets required).
* **Booking:** The amphitheater can be booked for private school performances or non-profit events.

## 2. Venue Rules
* **Seating:** First-come, first-served basis. Residents are encouraged to bring their own mats/cushions.
* **Food:** Outside food is allowed, but strictly no littering.
* **Smoking:** The entire amphitheater area is a strict "No-Smoking" zone.
