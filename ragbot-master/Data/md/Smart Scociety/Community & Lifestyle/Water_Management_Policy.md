# 💧 Water Usage & Conservation Policy

Rules established to prevent water scarcity and ensure its sustainable use.

## 1. Usage Guidelines
| Activity | Regulation | Penalty for Violation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Car Wash** | Use of buckets only (Hosepipes are prohibited) | Rs. 1,000 |
| **Gardening** | Watering allowed only during morning or evening hours | Rs. 500 |
| **Tank Overflow** | Automatic water level sensors are mandatory | Rs. 2,000 |

## 2. Technical SOPs
* **Motor Capacity:** Installation of water pumps exceeding 1 HP requires prior approval.
* **Boring:** Private boring/drilling for water is strictly prohibited; residents must use the society's water supply.
