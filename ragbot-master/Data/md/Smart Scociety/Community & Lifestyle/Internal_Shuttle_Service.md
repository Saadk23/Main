# 🚌 Internal Shuttle & Golf Cart Service

To reduce traffic and pollution, the society provides an internal electric shuttle service for easy commuting between sectors.

## 1. Route & Stops
* **Key Stops:** Main Gate, Commercial Area, Central Park, Mosque, and Hospital.
* **Frequency:** Shuttles run every 15 minutes during peak hours and every 30 minutes during off-peak hours.

## 2. Fare & Accessibility
| User Type | Per Trip Fee | Monthly Pass |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Students (Uniform) | Free | N/A |
| Senior Citizens | Free | N/A |
| General Residents | Rs. 30 | Rs. 500 |

* **Safety Note:** Children under 10 years are not allowed to travel alone on the shuttle service.
