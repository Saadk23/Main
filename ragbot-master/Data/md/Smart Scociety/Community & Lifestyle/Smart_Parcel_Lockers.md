# 📦 Smart Parcel Locker System

To ensure the safety of your online deliveries, we provide 24/7 automated parcel lockers at the main community gates.

## 1. How It Works
1. **Delivery:** Delivery riders drop the parcel into the secure locker using the resident's name/flat number.
2. **Notification:** The resident receives a unique OTP (One-Time Password) on the Society App.
3. **Collection:** Residents can pick up their parcels at any time by entering the OTP.

## 2. Rules & Storage
* **Retention Time:** Parcels must be collected within 48 hours. After this, a storage fee of **Rs. 100 per day** applies.
* **Restrictions:** No perishable items (food/groceries) or hazardous materials are allowed in lockers.
