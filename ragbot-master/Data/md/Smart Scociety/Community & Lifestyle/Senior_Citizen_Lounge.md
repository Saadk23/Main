# 🧓 Senior Citizen Lounge (The Heritage Club)

A dedicated space designed for the elderly residents of the society to socialize and relax.

## 1. Amenities
* **Indoor Games:** Chess, Carrom, and Card tables.
* **Reading Area:** Large-print newspapers and spiritual books.
* **Medical Assistance:** A dedicated nurse is present during lounge hours for BP and sugar monitoring.

## 2. Lounge Protocol
* **Age Requirement:** Access is reserved for residents aged 60 and above.
* **Timings:** 10:00 AM to 08:00 PM Daily.
* **Guest Policy:** One younger family member is allowed to accompany the senior resident as an assistant.
