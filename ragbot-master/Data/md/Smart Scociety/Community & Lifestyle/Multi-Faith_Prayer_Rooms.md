# 🙏 Multi-Faith Prayer & Meditation Rooms

While the central mosque serves our primary needs, the society provides dedicated silent spaces for meditation and residents of other faiths.

## 1. Usage & Access
* **Meditation Hall:** Open 24/7 for yoga, mindfulness, and silent prayer.
* **Prayer Rooms:** Specifically designated rooms for minority faith groups to perform their religious rites.

## 2. Code of Conduct
* **Respect:** All residents must maintain the sanctity and silence of these areas.
* **No Propagation:** Religious propagation or distribution of materials is strictly prohibited inside these communal spaces.
* **Maintenance:** Shoes must be kept in the outside racks provided at the entrance.
