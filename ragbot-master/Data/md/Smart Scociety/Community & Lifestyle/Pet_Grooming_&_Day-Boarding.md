# 🐾 Pet Grooming & Day-Boarding Center

To assist pet owners, the society provides a professional grooming and temporary boarding facility within the community premises.

## 1. Services Offered
* **Full Grooming:** Includes bathing, hair trimming, nail clipping, and ear cleaning.
* **Day-Boarding:** Professional supervision for pets while owners are at work or away for the day.
* **Pet Training:** Basic obedience training sessions conducted by certified trainers on weekends.

## 2. Service Charges & Timings
| Service | Duration / Unit | Charges (Residents) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Wash & Dry** | Per Session | Rs. 1,500 |
| **Full Grooming Kit** | Per Session | Rs. 3,500 |
| **Day-Boarding** | Per Day (8 AM - 8 PM) | Rs. 2,000 |
| **Pet Training** | 4-Week Course | Rs. 10,000 |

## 3. Mandatory Requirements
* **Vaccination:** All pets must have up-to-date vaccination records on file at the center.
* **Health Check:** Pets with contagious diseases or parasites (like ticks/fleas) will not be accepted for boarding until cleared by a vet.
* **Feeding:** Owners must provide the pet's preferred food for boarding services to avoid digestive issues.

## 4. Operational Hours
* **Monday - Saturday:** 09:00 AM to 07:00 PM
* **Sunday:** 10:00 AM to 04:00 PM (Grooming by appointment only)
