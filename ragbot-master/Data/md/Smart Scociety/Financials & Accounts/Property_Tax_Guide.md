# 🏛️ Property Tax & Society Dues Guide

This document outlines the tax obligations for property owners as per government regulations and society bylaws.

## 1. Types of Taxes & Levies
| Tax Type | Frequency | Calculation Basis |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Provincial Property Tax** | Annual | Plot size and rental value |
| **Society Development Cess** | One-time | Applied at the time of construction |
| **Water & Conservancy Tax** | Monthly | Fixed based on plot category |

## 2. Payment Deadlines
* **Early Bird Discount:** 5% discount if the annual property tax is paid before September 30th.
* **Exemptions:** Widows, orphans, and disabled persons may apply for tax rebates via the Admin Office.
