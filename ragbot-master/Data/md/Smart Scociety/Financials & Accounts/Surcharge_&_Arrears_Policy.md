# 💸 Surcharge & Arrears Policy

To ensure the smooth operation of society services, timely payment of dues is mandatory.

## 1. Late Payment Penalties
* **Grace Period:** Payments are due by the 10th of every month.
* **Late Fee:** A flat surcharge of **Rs. 500** or **10%** (whichever is higher) is applied after the due date.
* **Long-term Arrears:** Accounts overdue by more than 90 days will face utility suspension (Water/Security Tags).

## 2. Recovery Process
| Overdue Period | Action Taken |
| :--- | :--- |
| 30 Days | SMS & Email Reminder |
| 60 Days | Final Legal Notice |
| 90 Days | Disconnection of Services & Entry Block |
