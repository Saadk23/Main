# 📑 Property Transfer & Ownership Laws

Regulations regarding the purchase, sale, and transfer of property ownership within the society.

## 1. Transfer Procedure
| Step | Action Required | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **NDC Application** | Obtaining a No Dues Certificate | Seller |
| **Document Submission** | Submission of the Original Allotment Letter | Seller & Buyer |
| **Transfer Fee** | Payment of the prescribed transfer fees | Buyer |
| **Membership** | Acquisition of a new membership | Buyer |

## 2. Legal Requirements
* **Biometric Verification:** Biometric verification of both parties is mandatory at the time of transfer.
* **Taxes:** All government-imposed taxes (Capital Gain Tax/Stamp Duty) must be paid by the respective parties.
* **Succession:** In case of inheritance, a Succession Certificate from a competent court is required for transfer.
