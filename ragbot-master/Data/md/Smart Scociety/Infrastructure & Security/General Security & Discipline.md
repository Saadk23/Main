# 🛡 General Security & Discipline

The following regulations are in place to ensure the safety and security of the society and its residents.

## 🚗 Traffic & Parking Rules
1. **Speed Limit:** The maximum speed limit inside the society is **30 km/h**. Exceeding this limit is strictly prohibited.
2. **Parking:** Parking vehicles on the road is not allowed. Residents must always park their vehicles in their garage or designated parking bays.
3. **Guest Entry:** Every guest must register at the main gate upon entry. Residents are advised to generate a "Guest Code" via the "Society App" for a hassle-free entry process.
