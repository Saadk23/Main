# 🛍 Commercial Area & Market Regulations

## 1. Business Hours
* All markets must close by **11:00 PM** (Excluding Pharmacies and Medical Clinics).

## 2. Encroachment
| Area | Regulation | Penalty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Footpath** | Displaying goods on sidewalks is strictly prohibited | Shop Sealing |
| **Parking** | Shopkeepers must not block roads or public parking bays | Rs. 2,000 |
