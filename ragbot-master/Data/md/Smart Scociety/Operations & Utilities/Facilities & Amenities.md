# 🏟 Community Facilities & Amenities (Expanded)

The Society provides the following facilities to enhance the lifestyle and well-being of its members.

## 🌳 Public Parks & Playgrounds
Specific parks are available for every sector, along with a central "Grand Park" for all residents.
* **Timings:** Open from 04:30 AM (Fajr) until 11:00 PM.
* **Jogging Tracks:** Tracks are reserved exclusively for pedestrians. Bicycles and pets are strictly prohibited on the tracks.
* **Events:** Small birthday parties (maximum 20 guests) are permitted, provided the area is cleared of trash and noise levels remain minimal.

## 🕌 Mosque & Community Center
* **Mosque Etiquette:** Blowing horns near the Mosque is strictly prohibited. Please ensure vehicles are parked only within designated parking lines.
* **Community Hall Booking:** For weddings or large functions, bookings must be made at least 2 weeks in advance. Booking fees will be determined based on the house size and the number of guests.
