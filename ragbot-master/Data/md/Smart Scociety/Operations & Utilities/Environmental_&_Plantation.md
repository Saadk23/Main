# 🌳 Green Society & Plantation Rules

## 1. Tree Plantation
* Every member is encouraged to plant and maintain at least one tree in front of their residence.

## 2. Prohibitions
* **Tree Cutting:** Cutting down trees without society permission will result in a fine of **Rs. 5,000** per tree.
* **Lawns:** Green belts outside houses must only be used for grass and flowers; permanent construction is forbidden.
