# 🐕 Pet Ownership & Safety Rules

## 1. Registration
* All pets (especially dogs) must be registered with the society office, and a valid vaccination certificate must be provided.

## 2. Safety Protocols
| Rule | Description | Fine |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Leash Rule** | Pets must be on a leash in parks or public areas | Rs. 1,000 |
| **Aggressive Breeds** | Muzzles are mandatory for specific breeds in public | Rs. 2,000 |
| **Poop Scooping** | Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets | Rs. 500 |
