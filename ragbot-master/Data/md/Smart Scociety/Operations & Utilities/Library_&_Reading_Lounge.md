# 📚 Public Library & Reading Lounge

The society provides a quiet environment for students and book lovers to foster a culture of learning.

## 1. Operating Hours & Access
| Day | Opening Time | Closing Time | Access Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Monday - Friday | 09:00 AM | 09:00 PM | Members Only |
| Saturday | 10:00 AM | 06:00 PM | Members & Guests |
| Sunday | Closed | Closed | Maintenance |

## 2. Membership & Rules
* **Subscription:** Monthly membership is **Rs. 500** for residents.
* **Silence Policy:** Strict silence must be maintained. Mobile phones must be on silent mode.
* **Issuance:** A maximum of 2 books can be issued for 14 days. A fine of **Rs. 50 per day** applies for late returns.
